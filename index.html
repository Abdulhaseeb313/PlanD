About Plan D

Plan D Tourism, which was founded in 2017,serves as a one-stop shop for all travel requirements. Our management and personnel are knowledgeable and have a wealth of experience working in the aviation sector. Our team is made up of IATA-qualified employees with knowledge in sales and marketing with the necessary tools to provide our loyal customers with exceptional service. Constant priority is placed on customer satisfaction. Plan D Tourism manages domestic and international reservations, ticketing, vacation packages, car and limo rentals. We provide some of the most affordable car and limousine rentals in the UAE. Since our company's founding in 2017, we have vowed to offer our clients effective 24-hour services.
